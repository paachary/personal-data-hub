# Bank Accounts Help

## Overview

Manage your personal bank accounts linked to banks in the Bank Master.

## Features

-   **Add** a bank account (select bank, enter account number and type)
-   **Edit** existing accounts
-   **Delete** accounts
-   Account types: `Savings`, `Current`, `NRE`, `NRO`

## Prerequisites

-   Sysadmin must have added the bank to **Bank Master** first before you can link an account.

## Tips

-   Each account is linked to a bank from the master list.
-   Only your own accounts are visible when logged in as a regular user.
